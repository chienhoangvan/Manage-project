Welcome to ChienHV's Library

Your task is to manage the library and assist readers in borrowing and returning books

Some rules in the library:

- Borrowing and returning books must be done directly at the library.

- Librarians are responsible for assisting readers in borrowing and returning books.

- In the event that a reader affects the library, the librarian has the right to take away the reader's card.

- After borrowing books, readers are responsible for keeping the books carefully, and are responsible for any damage from the books.

- If lost or damaged, readers need to compensate 1.5 times the original book price.
